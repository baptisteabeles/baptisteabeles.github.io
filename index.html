Hi, I am a PhD in Learning Theory at Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona. I am interested by Online Learning Theory and its connection with Statistical Learning Theory in particual in the setting of dependent data.

More specifically, I have been focusing in the use of Online Learning theoretical results to provide better statistical learning guarantees. My research interests also include: Bandits, PAC-Bayes, Graphs Theory, and more broadly learning theory.
